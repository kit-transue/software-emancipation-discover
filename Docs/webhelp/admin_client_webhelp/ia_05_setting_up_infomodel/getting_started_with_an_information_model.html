Getting Started With an Information Model

Using the DIScover client, you can query and interact with a compact and cross-referenced representation of your source code’s components and inter-relationships. This representation of data, called the information model, is generated in whole or in increments.

The model is the foundation for DIScover’s ability to rapidly access, navigate, represent graphically, interpret, answer your questions about your code, and more.

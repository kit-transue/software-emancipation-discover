                                      FUNCTIONAL AREAS FOR SYSTEM TESTING


The following is a grid of the various elements of CodeRover that require testing.   The introduction of additional languages makes the amount of time required for complete testing combinatoric.  Needless to say exhaustive testing becomes extremely expensive:

Total time for exhaustive testing all functionality on all platforms for all languages:                                                                                                     ~512 hours

 “General” indicates functionality that is commonly used and central to the product. The time required is as follows:

              Total time for system testing on two platforms (NT, Win2000) for one language:

                                                                                ~128 hours * 2 platforms =  ~256 hours

Total time for system testing on two platforms (NT, Win2000) for two languages:       ~256 hours * 2 languages         = ~512 hours

When the release is ready and the last of the defects repaired, the final requirement is general testing on the major platforms and smoke testing on the others.  This is to insure the quality of the final build.

Total time for Release Testing, General Functionality two platforms (NT, Win2000) for two languages

                                                  ~87.5 hours * 2 platforms* 2 languages =   ~350 hours
